Admissions

Iqra University offers admissions twice a year for both undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Currently, admissions are open for the following programs:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science
  • Bechelor of Software Engineeering
  • Bechelor of Artifical Intelligence
  • Bachelor of Media Studies
  • Bachelor of Feshion Designing
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Computer Science
  • PhD in Data Science

Below are the steps to apply:

  1. Visit the official website and create an account.
  2. Fill out the online application form with accurate details.
  3. Upload the required documents, including academic transcripts and ID proof.
  4. Pay the application processing fee.
  5. Schedule and appear for the admission test (if applicable).
  6. Await the final merit list and offer letter.
